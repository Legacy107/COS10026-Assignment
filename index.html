MP3 FORMAT

MP3 has been a coding format that has been around for decades, and since its initial standardisation in 1993 has been a consistent part of audio formatting. It's initial creation was led by Karlheinz Bradenburg of the Fraunhofer Society, and it is thanks to him and his group's efforts that MP3 has become the dominant global formatter that you have certainly used in your life either knowingly or unknowingly. Even in spite of other technologies rising up to contest this mammoth of a format, its still here to stay for a long time.
